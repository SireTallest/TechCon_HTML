Our History

TechCon was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate software engineers and tech enthusiasts who envisioned a platform where innovators could share ideas and inspire the next generation of technology leaders. What began as a small gathering of 200 attendees in a modest conference hall has grown into one of Africa's premier technology conferences, attracting over 5,000 participants annually from across the globe. Over the years, TechCon has evolved from focusing solely on software development to encompassing emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. The conference has hosted groundbreaking product launches, facilitated partnerships worth millions of dollars, and provided a launchpad for countless startups that have gone on to transform industries. From its humble beginnings to becoming a catalyst for technological innovation across the continent, TechCon continues to embody the spirit of collaboration, learning, and progress that defined its creation nearly a decade ago.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower innovators, developers, and technology leaders by providing a world-class platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and collaboration. We believe that technology has the power to solve humanity's greatest challenges, and our conference serves as a bridge connecting visionaries with the resources, mentorship, and community they need to turn ideas into reality. We are committed to promoting diversity and inclusion in the tech industry, ensuring that voices from all backgrounds are heard and valued. Through inspiring keynotes, hands-on workshops, and interactive panel discussions, TechCon fosters an environment where learning thrives and innovation flourishes. Our ultimate goal is to accelerate Africa's digital transformation by nurturing talent, encouraging entrepreneurship, and creating opportunities for the next generation of tech leaders to shape the future of technology on the continent and beyond.
